Electrostate is combination of two words, ELECTRO + STATE. Electro means charge and state means at rest. so in electrostate we study about charges at rest. In electrostatics we study about interaction between charges.
CHARGE : It is a property of matter due to which an objects exerts attractive or repulsive force.
It is of two types :
1) positive charge (+)
2) negative charge (-)

Like charge repel each other and Unlike charge attract each other.

Properties of charge :-
1) it is conserved i.e. charge cannot be created nor be destroyed, it can only be transferred from one body to another.
If an object is neutral then it will be having + ve and –ve charge of equal magnitude.
Neutrality of an object means net charge on the object is zero.


2) charge is Quantised Fundamental unit of charge = charge on an electron or proton = e =1.602*10-19 c
Charge on an object can be integrated multiple of charge on an electron. i.e. Q = +---ne where n is I (integer)


3) Invariance of charge : It doesn’t depend upon frame of references.
It doesn’t depend upon velocity, accln etc.

• METHODS OF CHARGING OF A BODY
We can charge a body by two methods:
1) Charging by friction or rubbing
2) Charging by induction
3) Charging by conduction


Charging of friction : when two different types of materials are rubbed against each other then transfer of electrons occurs from one body to another material.
Material which loosed electron will become +ve charge and materials which gain electrons will become –ve charge.
• Tribolectric series : Electron gaining tendency increases from top to bottom in tribolectric series.
• Human beings
• Glass
• Mica
• Nylon
• Wool
• Silk
• Paper
• Steel
• Rubber
• Silicon

Above series which starts from human and end with silicon is Tribolectric series.

• Charging by induction : The process of charging a neutral body by bringing a charged body near by it without making contact between the two bodies is known as Charging by Induction.
When a charged body is brought near a conducting body then redistribution of charge occurs in the conductor.
If a positivily charged body is brought near a conducting sphere then negative charge on the sphere appears near postivly charge body and positive charge on the sphere will be on the farthest side of the sphere.
